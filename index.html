Space Bubbles: Orbital Solar Management System
An interactive simulation demonstrating space-based solar reflection technology for global climate control. Navigate orbital bubble arrays to redirect solar radiation and manage Earth's temperature through strategic geo-engineering. This game explores the cutting-edge concept of space-based weather modification systems. This idea is inspired by the research being done here: https://senseable.mit.edu/space-bubbles/
Operation Stratosphere: Atmospheric Engineering
Take command of advanced atmospheric engineering operations in the stratosphere. Deploy weather control technologies, manage atmospheric pressure systems, and implement geo-engineering solutions to influence weather patterns. Experience the challenges and precision required for large-scale atmospheric manipulation. Inspired by the research available on Wikipedia here: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stratospheric_aerosol_injection
PRISM: NHS Primary Care Intelligence System
A comprehensive prototype for the NHS Primary Care Intelligence System, designed to improve healthcare accessibility and data-driven decision making. This interactive prototype demonstrates innovative approaches to primary care management and patient data visualization. Part of the 10DS Hackathon September 2025 project focused on improving access to primary care.
