SoTA Weather Control & Geo-engineering Hackathon Entry
May 31-June 1, 2025
Team Godar Family: Kalen, Mir & Tony Godar
Space Bubbles: Orbital Solar Management System
An interactive simulation demonstrating space-based solar reflection technology for global climate control. Navigate orbital bubble arrays to redirect solar radiation and manage Earth's temperature through strategic geo-engineering. This game explores the cutting-edge concept of space-based weather modification systems. This idea is inspired by the research being done here: https://senseable.mit.edu/space-bubbles/
Operation Stratosphere: Atmospheric Engineering
Take command of advanced atmospheric engineering operations in the stratosphere. Deploy weather control technologies, manage atmospheric pressure systems, and implement geo-engineering solutions to influence weather patterns. Experience the challenges and precision required for large-scale atmospheric manipulation.
